Antonyms Overview


Antonyms Overview:

Antonym(s) is a word or are words that mean the opposite of a given word. A word can have more than one antonym. If a word has more than one antonym, then it is not necessary that each of the antonyms will be used for the same purpose in the English language because the context decides which word is the best fit.

Word Meaning


Perhaps: Maybe; possibly.
Sentence: Perhaps we can go to the beach this weekend if the weather is nice.

Property: Something owned; a possession, such as land, buildings, or belongings.
Sentence: The family purchased a beautiful property in the countryside with a large garden and a cosy house.

Mountainside: The side or slope of a mountain.
Sentence: The hikers trekked up the steep mountainside, taking in the breathtaking views of the valley below.
